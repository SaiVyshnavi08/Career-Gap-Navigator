**The Problem**

65% of students graduate with skills gaps they didn't know they had. There are 3.8M unfilled tech jobs in the US, yet no tool connects what students learn in class to what employers actually want — until now.
**The Solution**

Career Gap Navigator is a multi-agent AI system that analyzes live job markets, identifies your skill gaps, and builds personalized learning paths — all in real time.
4 specialized AI agents collaborate autonomously:
AgentRoleTechnology🧠 Curriculum AnalyzerExtracts skills from courses, resume & manual inputGroq Llama 3.3 70B🔍 Job Market ScoutSearches real-time job postings (last 30 days)TheirStack API📊 Gap AnalyzerCompares student skills vs market demandsPython📚 Learning Path GeneratorCreates week-by-week study plans with free resourcesGroq Llama 3.3 70B
A Coordinator runs all 4 agents in sequence and compiles the final career report.

**Key Features**


**📄 Resume Upload** — PDF parsing extracts skills client-side (never leaves your browser)
**🔍 Live Job Data** — Real-time postings from 300K+ websites via TheirStack API
**📊 Match Percentage **— Every job shows your fit score with explainable matches
**🧠 AI Career Insight** — "You're 20% ready. Focus on AWS, Docker. Reach ~60% in 3 weeks"
**✅ Explainable Job Matching **— See exactly which skills match and which are missing per job
**📚 Personalized Learning Path** — Week-by-week plan with IBM SkillsBuild, Kaggle, Google, freeCodeCamp, fast.ai, Hugging Face & more
**🔒 Responsible AI** — Full transparency, no data stored, resume parsed locally
**🔄 Filters** — Remote only, Visa/Sponsorship, Experience level, Sort by match % or date
